About Jordan Brodie

Web Designer, Web Developer, Photographic Designer, and a Sports Fanatic.

Hello, my name is Jordan Brodie. I am a student currently enrolled in Saint Raymond High School For Boys, a Cathoic Private High School. I live in Bronx, New York and I am deeply interested into subjects such as computer programming, web development, photography and sports/sports media.

Throughout high school so far I have never failed a class and my best classes were Global Studies and Science. I have been in only honors classes for all of my subjects, and I also take Religion/Moral classes. During school, I had briefly been apart of the International Club, which is a club where a group of students throw parties for certain ethnic groups and celebrate those ethnic groups in unity as one group (which contain many Hispanic, Black and Asian/Indian races) I also had been apart of the Indoor and Outdoor Track team for the winter and spring.

I had learned about computer programming and grabbed a better understanding of computer science at a summer intensive program called All Star Code. Founded by Christina Lewis Halpern in 2013, it is a summer intensive program that teaches young men of ethnicity and color about cuomputer science. This program taught me roughly everything I need to know about Computer Science, and Web Development. It also made me understand better about education, college decision-making, entrprenuership and presentation.

I also like to do many other hobbies beside computer science. I like to take and edit photos and videos. Examples of my photos are further on my website on the Projects Page. Besides doing work on my laptop, I also like to play sports (mostly basketball and football) and hang out with my friends. I am a hardworking young man who has humor, but does the work when needed to.
